# Goals
-To help farmers improve crop productivity using AI-powered guidance.

-To provide easy access to soil, weather, and crop information.

-To reduce farming risks by offering quick pest and disease detection.

-To save farmers time by giving fast, reliable recommendations.

-To create a simple and user-friendly platform that even beginners can use.

# Research Insights
-Many farmers struggle with unpredictable weather, soil quality issues, and lack of reliable guidance.

-Existing farming apps are often too complex, filled with technical language, or not available in regional languages.

-Farmers want quick, simple, and action-focused advice instead of long scientific explanations.

-AI-based tools can help reduce crop loss by offering timely alerts and personalized suggestions.

# Problems With Existing Apps
-Complicated UI: Many apps have crowded screens and confusing layouts.

-Too much technical language: Hard for beginners or farmers with limited digital experience.

-Slow performance: Heavy apps lag on low-end phones.

-Generic advice: Many apps provide the same suggestions to everyone instead of personalized insights.

-Lack of animations/visual clarity: Makes the experience feel boring or difficult to navigate.
